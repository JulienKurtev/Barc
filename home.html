Online Animal Cruelty Prevention and Education Program

INSPIRING CHANGE THROUGH KNOWLEDGE: Benchmark Animal Rehabilitative Curriculum (B.A.R.C.) is designed to educate and rehabilitate individuals who have mistreated animals. This unique online course instills a respect for the needs of all animals and describes the potential consequences of failing to meet those needs. Learn more about the curriculum, key features of the course (including the use of facial recognition technology to ensure that the person referred to take the course is the one actually taking it), student eligibility requirements, and how to make a referral.
